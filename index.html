Who are We?

Nos De Republica fortis Engineers

We, the Republic Of Mighty Engineers (ROME), as practitioners of engineering, are trying to be professionals who can invent, design, analyze, build and test machines, complex systems, structures, gadgets and materials to fulfill functional objectives and requirements while considering the limitations imposed by practicality,regulation, safety and cost. We are full of enthusiasm and motivation and have a good social bonding with each other even though its just a week till we knew each other. The only thing we are lacking is the experience which we are gaining from HighRadius which we love and as we progress, we will prove ourselves to be the best of All!

Nos, Omnipotentem Reipublicae Engineers, ut medicos de engineering, quae sit doctorum, qui temptatis potuit vel invenire vel excogitandum, analyze, quaedam et machinis test, universa systems, structuris et materiae gadgets proposita et requisita iniunctum munus implere intuens limitibus prudentiam,ordinationem tutus essem. Nos plena mirandi et motum ducit et ad bonum sociale vinculum etiamsi ad se iustus a septimana donec gnaris, prodebantur. Solum rei est penuria sumus reportando experientia, quod nos ex qua HighRadius amamus, et ut proficiamus, ut et nos et ipsi probare optimus omnium.
